Level 1: Robot Bay

Drag and Drop game allowing customization of a robot chassis with a selection of parts.

Level 2: Processing

In processing, the player is tasked with sorting rocks based on coloration. Hovering over a crane will cause it to release its boulder. Be careful, the forman does not like it when rocks are mixed up! Be sure to drop each in the correct heap. Blue rocks are worth 38 points, Tan rocks are worth 57 points, and Gray rocks are worth 76 points. Getting a rock in the wrong heap costs you 19 points. Good Luck!

Level 3: Deep Core

Obstacle-avoidance game involving guiding a small robot through hazardous underground catacombs.
